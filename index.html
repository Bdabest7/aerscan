What the platform will include

Scope is evolving. Here’s the planned capability set for private beta.

Box (Autonomous dock)

Scheduled flights, safe launch windows, weather/health checks, remote ops.

Grid (Fleet & airspace)

Fleet manager, geofences, NOTAM/airspace context, telemetry and audit trails.

Cloud (Processing & QA)

LiDAR/RGB ingest, control checks, change detection, deliverables packaging.

Studio (Dashboards & alerts)

Viewer, annotations, thresholds, incident alerts, scheduled reports.

Integrations

Webhooks & exports to ArcGIS, QGIS, BIM, storage buckets, and APIs.

Security & Compliance

Role-based access, audit logs, and compliance-friendly data handling.
